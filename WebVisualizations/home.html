Overview

This website contains plots of different weather parameters measured in cities across many different latitudes. These plots show an interesting lack of strong correlations. Data points for Humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed seem evenly spread across the latitudes with no apparent scheme. However, the scatter plot of temperate shows an interesting symmetry about the equator, suggesting that any correlations that could be described linearly may be obscured by at the global level.
